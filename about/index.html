    the project

    The european banking union is a project under construction. The ultimate goal of this website is to gather and structure banking union-related documents, from legislative acts to research papers, while fostering the debate on those issues that are unfinished.

    In the current beta version of the web the reader will find different interviews with academics, researchers and professionals discussing some of these banking union topics.
